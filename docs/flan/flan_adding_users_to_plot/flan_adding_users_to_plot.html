/flan — Adding Users
// HOW TO SHARE YOUR PLOT //
01
OPEN THE FLAN MENU

Run /flan menu while standing inside your claimed plot. Once the menu opens, click Edit Permission Groups to manage who has access.

02
CHOOSE A ROLE

You'll see two trust levels: Visitor and Co-Owner. Visitors have limited interaction, while Co-Owners get full build and modify access.

03
ADD THE PLAYER

Click either Visitor or Co-Owner. At the top you'll see an anvil — click it, type the player's username, then take the book out of the anvil to confirm.

ALL DONE!
That player now has access to your plot. Have fun out there!
