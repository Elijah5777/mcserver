L LAND CLAIMING 1 command
/flan ▼
TIP To claim land, equip your Golden Hoe and select two diagonal corners of your plot.
/flan info Get information about a claimed plot.
/flan delete Remove the claim you're standing in. Only works inside your own plot.

T TELEPORTATION 2 commands
/warp ▼
/warp [name] Teleport to a predefined location.
/warp list List all available warp points.
/warp create [name] Create a new warp point. Admin only.
/home ▼
/sethome Set your personal home location.
/home Teleport back to your home.
/delhome Delete your home location.
