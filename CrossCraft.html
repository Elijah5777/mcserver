CrossCraft Commands

/flan

To claim land, equip your Golden Hoe and select two diagonal corners of your plot.

/flan info — Get information about a claimed plot.

/flan delete — Remove the claim that you are in. (Only works in YOUR plot)

To allow a player to access your plot, click here for detailed instructions.

/warp

/warp [name] — Warp to a predefined location.

/warp list — List all available warp points.

/warp create [name] — Create a new warp point (admin only).

/home

/sethome — Set your personal home location.

/home — Teleport back to your home.

/delhome — Delete your home location.
